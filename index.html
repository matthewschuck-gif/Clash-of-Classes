The Wheel

Spin to earn bonus squad points

The Wheel is one of the most exciting parts of Clash of Classes — a high-stakes, multi-ring spinning challenge where students can earn big bonus points for their squad. Every spin is a choice: bank your points or risk it all to go deeper toward the center for a chance at 100 points!

How to Earn a Spin
There are four ways to get called to the Wheel:
💵
Trait Dough Drawing
Every Friday, 5 names are drawn from the Trait Dough bin. The more Trait Dough you turn in, the more entries you have. Winners are called to the lobby to spin.
🏆
Competition Winners
Winning a Clash of Classes competition or event can earn your squad Wheel spins as part of the prize.
🌟
Student of the Month
Students recognized as Student of the Month may earn a Wheel spin as part of their celebration.
⚡
Surprise Moments
Surprise Wheel spins can happen anytime — at lunch, assemblies, or faculty meetings for staff. You never know when your name might be called!
How the Wheel Works
The Wheel has multiple rings — outer to center. You always start on the outer ring. The further in you go, the higher the stakes.

Start on the Outer Ring
Every spinner begins on Ring 1 — the outer ring. Give it a spin and see where it lands.
2
Land on a Number? Bank It or Risk It
If you land on a space showing a number and a down arrow ↓, you have a choice: keep those points for your squad and walk away, or risk them and spin the next ring inward for a chance at more. You can risk up to 4 times total to work your way to the center.
3
Make It to the Center — Squad Color = 100 Points
If you risk all the way to the center ring and your spin lands on your squad's color, your squad earns 100 points! If it lands on a different color, you earn nothing. Big risk, big reward.
Special Spaces
Watch out for these special spaces on the wheel — they change the rules!
LOYALTY
You only earn the points if you are on your squad's color section. If you land on Loyalty but it's not your squad's color, you earn nothing. Loyalty rewards the home team!
GIVE
Landing here means you must give those points to another squad instead of keeping them for yourself. A generous — or unlucky — space depending on how you look at it!
AUTO
No choice here — you automatically advance to the next ring inward. You don't get to decide whether to risk it; you must spin again. Auto can push you closer to the center whether you're ready or not!

Trait Dough

EMS schoolwide token economy system

Trait Dough is a schoolwide token economy system that highlights individual students demonstrating Life Ready Graduate Traits in action.

Ways to Earn

Students can earn Trait Dough through three pathways: In Action (Individual recognition), Clash of Classes Competition (Team), and Mountaineer Pride (Team).

The Process

Staff members are provided "Trait Dough" to give to students who exemplify different LRG traits. When students "Be the E" by demonstrating ANY Life Ready Graduate trait, the adult recognizes their actions by providing the physical or digital Trait Dough with the reason for earning.

The adult immediately circles the trait recognized and writes/signs their name on the back. The student may collect, save, or turn in their Trait Dough at any point — during lunch, PASS, or the beginning/end of the school day.

Once in the office, the student explains why they are there and turns in their Trait Dough. They are then entered into a drawing to receive a "gnarly prize" during period drawings. Winners are announced through the morning announcements.

Principles

Purposeful — Each adult may give as little or as much Trait Dough each day.
Sustainable — Printed templates can be found in the workroom and a digital copy is available in the links section.
Equitable — Using data to log what trait, who is giving, and who is receiving is vital to ensure fair distribution.
Manageable — Make it your own. Don't stress over how much or when you distribute Trait Dough. Look for authentic opportunities.

Student of the Month Connection

Both Trait Dough and Student of the Month highlight students demonstrating LRG Traits, but in different ways. Trait Dough can happen anytime from any adult, while Student of the Month recognizes 2 students per team each month for exemplifying the elevated trait of the month. Selected students are invited to a special monthly experience.

Student of the Month

A designed experience celebrating LRG traits

Student of the Month is a designed recognition experience at Ephrata Middle School. Each month, 24 students are selected — 2 per squad — for exemplifying the elevated Life Ready Graduate trait of that month. Selected students are invited to a special community experience to celebrate the trait they demonstrated.

👥
Who is Selected
24 students total each month — 2 per squad (Black, Gold, Grey, Purple) — selected by their squad team for exemplifying the trait of the month.
📅
When
Selections are made at the end of each month. The recognition experience typically takes place from 2:10–2:55 PM in the EMS Media Center.
🌟
The Experience
Each month features a unique community partnership — students celebrate their trait through hands-on experiences with local businesses, organizations, and community leaders.
How Teams Select Students
1
Collaborate with Your Team
Squad team members discuss which students have most authentically exemplified the trait of the month. Look for genuine moments — not just academic performance.
2
Submit via the CoC Landing Page
Use the Student of the Month nomination form on the Clash of Classes Landing Page. Select 2 students from your squad and include a brief reason for each.
3
Students Are Invited
Selected students receive their certificate and a personal message from their team explaining why they were chosen. They are then invited to the monthly experience.
4
Squad Points Awarded
Each Student of the Month selection earns 5 points for your squad. These points are added to the live tracker and count toward the overall standings.
Community Partners & Experiences
Each month's experience is connected to the LRG trait and features a local community partner. Experiences take place 2:10–2:55 PM.
